01. About Me

Hi, I’m Engy Muhammed, a web developer passionate about creating modern, responsive, and user-friendly websites. My journey started with a simple curiosity every time I came across a well designed website, I wanted to understand what makes it feel smooth, clean, and professional. That curiosity turned into a real passion, and since then I’ve been consistently learning, building, and improving through hands-on projects. I focus on building interfaces that combine clean UI, strong structure, and solid performance. I care about the details that make a real difference such as responsive layouts, consistency, accessibility, and a seamless user experience. I aim to write code that’s not only functional, but also maintainable and scalable. What makes my work unique is that I don’t just “build pages” I build digital experiences that represent the brand properly and feel effortless to use. I enjoy turning ideas into real products, and I’m always excited to take on new challenges and collaborate on meaningful projects. If you’re looking for someone who values both design and functionality, I’d love to connect.

Fast-forward to today, and I've had the privilege of working at an advertising agency, a start-up, and a huge corporation.
